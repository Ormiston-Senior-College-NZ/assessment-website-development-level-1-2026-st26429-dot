Join the Club

Be part of a vibrant, welcoming cultural community.

Who Can Join?

All students at Ormiston Senior College are welcome to join CID. You do not need to be Indian — anyone interested in Indian culture can be part of the club.

Student Leadership Opportunities

  • Event organisers
  • Dance and music leaders
  • Media and promotions team
  • Assembly presenters and hosts

Teacher in Charge

The club is supported by a teacher in charge who helps plan events, organise resources, and connect with the wider school community.
